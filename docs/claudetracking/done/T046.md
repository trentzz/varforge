# T046: Add `normal.tumour_contamination` field; apply somatic mutations at low level in normal simulation

**Epic**: EPIC-TOOLING (overallplans/EPIC-TOOLING.md)
**Priority**: low
**Depends on**: T034
**Status**: todo

## Goal

A `normal.tumour_contamination` config field causes the normal sample to contain a low level of somatic mutation signal, simulating tumour-in-normal contamination.

## Steps

1. Add `normal.tumour_contamination: <float>` to the paired config schema.
2. In the normal simulation path, spike in somatic variants at `contamination_fraction × normal_coverage` effective coverage.
3. Exclude contamination reads from the germline truth VCF but note them in the simulation report.

## Notes

Contamination fraction × normal coverage → somatic reads in normal sample. Depends on T034.
